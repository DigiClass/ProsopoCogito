This is just a dummy text to get you started with Recogito's text annotation view.
Create annotations by selecting text with your mouse. We have already created an
example commentary annotation for you here. Click it to open it.

You can tag words or phrases as places. Recogito will automatically provide a
suggestion for your selection, which you can confirm or change manually. For example,
try selecting the word Vienna, and click the "Place" category button in the editor popup.

Ready to go? Upload your own text documents by clicking the blue 'New Document' button
